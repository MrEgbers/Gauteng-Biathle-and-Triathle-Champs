Rules

Qualification Criteria:

  • Be fully affiliated to SAMPA and the Province.

  • Pay the full registration fee.

Meeting these criteria is essential for participation in the championships.

General Biathle & Triathle Rules:

The competition will adhere to the latest UIPM Biathle/Triathle rules and regulations. Key aspects include:

  • Swimming: All participants must be competent swimmers.
  • Running: Competitors will complete designated running distances.
  • Shooting (for Triathle): Participants in Triathle will also complete a laser shooting leg. Specific pistol and target requirements will be strictly enforced.
  • Transitions: Efficient transitions between disciplines are crucial.
  • Fair Play: All participants are expected to demonstrate sportsmanship and adherence to rules.

A detailed rulebook can be requested via email or will be available at the event briefing.

Important Race Rules & Distances:

  • The "lap rule" may be enforced during the running and swimming portions of the event. Any competitor who is lapped by the leading participant in their heat may be eliminated from the race. This is for safety and to maintain race flow.
  • For detailed distances per age category for Biathle and Triathle, please refer to the official UIPM Competition Rules document:
